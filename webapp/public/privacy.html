Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2025

Program Name

Anti-Theft System SMS Service

Owner and Operator

This service is operated by Gonnie Ben-Tal, sole proprietor, as part of Wayne State University UROP project HP0787.

Information We Collect

When you opt in to the Anti-Theft System SMS Service, we collect the following information:

  • Phone number
  • Consent timestamp
  • Opt-out status
  • Device-generated event metadata (GPS coordinates, timestamps, photos) that may be referenced in SMS messages

How We Use Your Information

Mobile information is used solely for delivering Anti-Theft System alerts and command replies to the device owner. This includes intrusion alerts, status updates, and command confirmations.

Information Sharing

Mobile numbers are never shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes. No information sharing with third parties or affiliates occurs for marketing purposes.

Message Frequency

0–2 messages per week during normal operation, plus event-driven alerts.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-Out

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message received from the Anti-Theft System. Once you opt out, all outbound SMS from the system will be disabled for your number.

Help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us by email at gonnie2219@gmail.com.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, contact: gonnie2219@gmail.com
