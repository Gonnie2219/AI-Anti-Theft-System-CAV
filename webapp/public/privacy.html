Program Name

Gonnie Ben-Tal Anti-Theft

Owner and Operator

This service is operated by Gonnie Ben-Tal, sole proprietor, as part of Wayne State University UROP project HP0787.

Information We Collect

When you opt in to the Gonnie Ben-Tal Anti-Theft program, we collect the following information:

  • Mobile phone number
  • Vehicle GPS location
  • Sensor and event data (vibration, door open/close, motion)
  • Photos captured by the onboard camera
  • Timestamps of all events and messages

How We Use Your Information

All information collected is used solely to deliver automated security alerts and status notifications to the device installer. This includes intrusion alerts, GPS location updates, status reports, and command confirmations.

Information Sharing

Your phone number and SMS opt-in consent are not shared with any third parties or affiliates. Your information is not used for marketing purposes.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies and depends on vehicle activity.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-Out

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message received from the Gonnie Ben-Tal Anti-Theft program. Once you opt out, all outbound SMS from the system will be disabled for your number.
