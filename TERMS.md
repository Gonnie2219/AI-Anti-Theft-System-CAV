# Terms and Conditions — AI-Enabled Vehicle Anti-Theft System

**Program name:** AI-Enabled Unauthorized Access Detection for Connected and Autonomous Vehicles

**Description:** This program sends SMS alert messages to the owner of an anti-theft device when sensors on the device detect a possible vehicle intrusion event. The owner may also send command messages back to the device to control its state remotely.

**Message frequency:** Event-driven. Typical frequency is 0–2 messages per week during normal operation. Frequency may be higher during an active intrusion event.

**Message and data rates:** Standard message and data rates may apply, per the recipient's mobile carrier plan.

**HELP:** Reply **HELP** to receive a list of supported commands and contact information.

**STOP:** Reply **STOP** to opt out of all SMS from this system. After opting out, you will receive no further messages until you re-enroll.

**Support:** For support, contact gonnie2219@gmail.com.

**Eligibility:** This program is operated as a single-owner research prototype. The only recipient is the device owner, who is also the operator of the program.
